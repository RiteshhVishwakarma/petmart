💡 WHY IT WASN'T WORKING:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

navigation? (optional) means:
- navigation might be undefined
- navigation?.navigate() might not execute
- No error thrown, just silently fails

navigation (required) with useNavigation means:
- navigation is always defined
- navigation.navigate() always executes
- Proper navigation guaranteed
